<align=center><size=130%>Plants VS. Zombies: Fusion - Update 3.3.1 Changelog</size></align>

1. New Mode - Starbound Adventure
- Accessed through the Challenge Mode menu.
- Progress through the main story to unlock basic plants. Tackle side quests to unlock upgrade plants (purple cards) and utility plants (white cards). Strategically combine task reward effects to conquer high-difficulty stages!
- This mode features 5 chapters (Day - Roof), each containing 6 main story levels and 6 side story levels. Complete the main story and collect 6 stars from the side story to unlock the next chapter.
- In the main story, choose between two tasks to complete and earn rewards. You can rebeat levels to unlock both rewards and switch between them.
- Stars earned from side missions can be used to unlock Star Upgrades.
- Zombies may appear in higher tiers. Each higher tier increases their attack power and toughness, and the further you progress the higher tier zombies you will encounter.
- *Note: You are recommended to complete the normal Adventure mode first before progressing to Starbound Adventure.

5. New Map - Lava
- 6 Lane Map: Lanes 1, 2, 5 and 6 are exposed volcanic rock; lanes 3 and 4 are lava lanes.
- Aquatic plants can be placed on lava but will take continuous damage.
- Every 60 seconds, lava in a random location will erupt, dealing massive damage to plants in a 3x3 area. Ice-shrooms can suppress lava activity.

6. New Level - The Gods 8: Trial of Acclimation
- Initial Modifiers:
  - Vulcannon + Epic Twin Inferno-nut Synergy
  - Both Cornveyor Weed modifiers
- Use the given plants to kill zombies for sun that can be used to upgrade your plants or revive lost ones.
- Defeat the Vengeful Archduke and fight your way to victory!
- *Note: Plants are significantly buffed in this level.

7. New Chibi Boss
- Purchase a new modifier to unlock the Iceborg Prototype and have her fight alongside you in your Odyssey runs!

8. Balance Adjustments
- The "Ember" debuff now enhances other status effects.
  - Ember + Chill: Reduces zombie speed by an additional 10%
  - Ember + Poison: Poison duration becomes infinite
  - Ember + Enflamed: Damage increase from enflamed is increased by 10%
