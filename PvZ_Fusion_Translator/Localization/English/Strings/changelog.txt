<align=center><size=130%>Plants VS. Zombies: Fusion - Update 3.4.1 Changelog</size></align>

1. New Levels - I, Zombie Adventure
- Accessible via the Puzzle Mode's Switch Level Group button.
- Includes 3 chapters of rich levels and tutorials to guide you step by step.

2. New Odyssey Mode - Odyssey: Investment
- Based on Odyssey: Purgatory. Wave-spawned zombies no longer grant points, and free plant modifier draws are removed. Instead:
- You start with a large amount of points and earn more points after each round and can open the Odyssey Shop every round.
- Epic Odyssey Modifiers are sold at discounted prices. Collect enough to unlock Affinities with extra bonuses.
- New Investment Modifiers: Selectable once per Map. Choosing Gold or Diamond-tier Investment Modifiers increases the base damage of Affinities, but raises high-tier zombie spawn rates and grants zombies extra damage reduction.

3. Odyssey Mode Overhaul
- Odyssey Shop now refresh with 4 Epic Odyssey Unlocks + 4 random Modifiers.
- Post-round random Modifier selection improved: if a particular type of Common Odyssey plant is on the lawn, its Modifiers appear more often (shop chances unchanged).
- Removed the requirement to pick 10 Common Odyssey Plants at the start; all are unlocked by default
- Cursed Purgatory and River Route now use separate entrances and save slots.
- Optimized Odyssey save file format (Modifier data from old saves no longer compatible).
- Added exclusive HP bars for all Mini-boss Zombies (toggleable on the main menu).
- Improved Common Odyssey Modifier sorting order.

4. New Gacha Mode Options
- Accessible from the Main Menu
- Epic Odyssey Zombies, Mini-bosses, and Evolved Zombies are now toggleable.
- If all three are enabled, Odyssey Plants have a 10% chance to become their Stellar variants.

5. New Plants
- Basic Fusions:
  * Rebound-shroom (Fume-shroom + Cabbage-pult)
  * Squish (Puff-shroom + Squash)
  * Doom Pumpkin (Doom-shroom + Pumpkin)
  * Spike Pot (Spikeweed + Flower Pot)
  * Lumos Pea (Peashooter + Plantern)
  * Squash Drone (Squash + Blover)
  * Coldslaw (Ice-shroom + Cabbage-pult)
- Odyssey Fusions:
  = Common:
    * Boreas Umbrella (Pharos Umbrella + Ice-shroom)
    * Quasar (Omni Pumpkin[renamed from Mechanized Pumpkin] + Starfruit)
    * Cornposter Pot (Cornveyor Weed + Flower Pot)
  = Epic:
    * Cryolina Reaper
    
6. New Zombies
- Common:
  * Chameleon Football Zombie
  * Charger Football Zombie
- Special: Alpha Assassin Squad are now Omega Assassin Squad when appearing as Mini-boss and it now has a separate ID.

7. New Skins
- Bloomaggedon skin for Shroomaggedon

8. New Chibi Boss
- Purchase a new modifier to unlock Baby Jill and have her fight alongside you in your Odyssey runs!

9. New QoL
- Added "Skip Plant Selection" option in Endless modes.
- Added hotkey B to open/close the Plant Selection screen (if applicable).
- Optimized underlying code to reduce lag, especially when zombie HP overlay are disabled.

10. Balance Adjustments
- Giga Mecha-nut: now limited to healing up to 10M HP per round.

9. Bug fixes
- Fixed an issue where some Zombotanies couldn't be stunned by butter due to being unable to find their heads.
- Fixed various other bugs.
